H&M Personalized Fashion Recommendations

After coming across the Kaggle competition for H&M recommendation system I decided to join it and give recommendation systems another shot. I had previously worked on a movie recommendation system couple of year ago and thought this would be interesting to work on a similar project and compare my progress.

I started by doing an elementary EDA on the dataset. I uploaded the analysis jupyter file to github. In the future I will be uploading the entire project when the competition is done.

My approach to this competition was to try out simple ideas first such as recommending popular personal items, recommending similar items and trending weekly/monthly items. This resulted in a score of around .0200 with highest currently being .0330

For the next step I will be splitting the recommendation two modules, retrieval and ranking and applying different techniques and strategy in each one. This is still work in development and my approach might change in the future. I will be updating this page periodically with progress if necessary
